🙋 About Me

Vann is currently a 2023 undergraduate student majoring in Communication Engineering at the School of Informatics, Xiamen University (XMU). My current research interests include multimodal large language models(MLLMs), audio-related recognition and understanding, agents, and XAI.

My ongoing work focuses on enhancing the adaptability of models in real-world scenarios for further emotional comprehension and analysis through the preliminary recognition of multimodal audio content. As a beginner in scientific research, I am still in the learning stage and am currently striving to master professional knowledge and explore cutting-edge research.
